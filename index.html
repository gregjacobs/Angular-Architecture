Welcome to my site for Angular guides. These are general guides about AngularJS development, architecture, testing, and general best practices developed over the years, and which have proven to be highly effective for writing large software applications.

Note: This is so much a site for Angular tutorials or getting started guides, but rather one for effectively building large, maintainable, and testable applications with AngularJS. These guides may not quite make sense just yet if you're just learning Angular, but come back once you have understood the basics of Angular and are now taking on a project of sufficient size (7-10+ pages, components, etc.).

Please feel free to ask questions, contribute your own ideas (I'm always looking to make things better), and even pull request changes to the site on the GitHub repo.
